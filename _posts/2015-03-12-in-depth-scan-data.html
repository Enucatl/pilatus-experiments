The setup is a new 120 keV design energy, 3rd Talbot order interferometer. The gratings have been fabricated at KIT.

The gratings had to be realigned after the installation of the pilatus detector, hence a direct comparison with the mythen data cannot be performed on a pixel-by-pixel basis. The results are nevertheless consistent in showing a rather constant visibility of around 5%. The part with lower visibility moves around as the gratings are moved and should be due to a residual misalignment.
